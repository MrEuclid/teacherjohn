Alpha Particle Lab

Find the 4 atoms hidden inside the box using as few shots as possible!

How to Play

Click the outer gray border to fire a particle. Deduce the atoms' locations and click inside the box to guess.

Signal Legend
  • H (Hit): Direct impact.
  • R (Reflect): Head-on forcefield bounce.
  • Num: Deflected 90° by nearby atom.
Efficiency Target
  • ★ Gold: ≤ 14 shots
  • ★ Silver: 15 - 22 shots
  • ★ Bronze: 23+ shots
