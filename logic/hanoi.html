Tower of Hanoi

Logic Puzzle for Young Explorers

Difficulty:
Moves 0
PLAY

How to play

Click a tower to pick up the top disk. Then click a different tower to drop it. Your goal is to move the whole stack to the last tower!

STOP

The Main Rule

You can only put a smaller disk on top of a larger one. Never put a big disk on a tiny one!
